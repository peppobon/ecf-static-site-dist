Topics Series Journals

Annali di Ca’ Foscari. Serie occidentale

The journal continues the tradition of the  Annali di Ca’ Foscari, founded in 1962, collecting the occidentalistica inheritance. It is an expression of the Department of Language and Cultural Studies comparative University Ca’ Foscari University of Venice and wants to represent the different disciplinary souls: the literary and philological studies, linguistic and language teaching studies, historical studies.

Annali di Ca’ Foscari. Serie orientale

The mission of Annali di Ca’ Foscari - Serie orientale is to foster original and interdisciplinary research in the fields of Asian and North African Studies. Each annual issue features articles and reviews written by leading scholars whose contributions span across a vast array of topics.

Archivio d’Annunzio

A journal that considers the figure of Gabriele d'Annunzio as an eponymous sample of an interdisciplinary and multicultural vision. By analizyng not only the poet's personality, but also that of other characters who followed his heels, it creates a humanistic comparison which covers a large and culturally significant period of time and a wide range of fields: from literature and cinema, to political activism and cultural habits.

EL.LE

Quarterly magazine of the Centre for Research on Language Teaching, Department of Linguistic and Cultural Comparative Studies, University Ca 'Foscari Venezia.

English Literature

English literature promotes a multidisciplinary discussion of the several aspects of English literature and the literatures in English language.

Il Tolomeo

rom the beginning Il Tolomeo  found room for postcolonial voices from the Francophone world, a space which was shared with writers using English.

MDCCC 1800

The journal aims to expand the field of studies in an interdisciplinary perspective considering the plots and the relationships between shapes art in the macroscopic framework of the European nineteenth century.

Quaderni Veneti

The journal will collect researches (written in all of the Western European culture languages) with philological, literary, linguistic and cultural-history nature related to the textual and cultural tradition of the Triveneto, from the Middle Ages to contemporary times.

Rassegna iberistica

Each issue publishes articles, notes and reviews, divided between the Spanish-American, Luso-Brazilian, and Catalan linguistic and cultural areas.

Ricerche giuridiche

The journal, which has biannual periodicity, except for the publication of any supplement, is an expression of the Doctoral Project of Higher Education in Legal Sciences and Legal Studies Center of the Economics Department of the University Ca’ Foscari Venice.

Venezia Arti

Venezia Arti is an annual journal, founded in 1987 by Giuseppe Mazzariol and Wladimiro Dorigo as bulletin of the then Department of the history and criticism of the arts.
