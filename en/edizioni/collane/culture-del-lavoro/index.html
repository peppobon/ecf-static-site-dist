Culture del lavoro

  • TopicSociology 
  • Head office Università Ca’ Foscari Dipartimento di Studi Umanistici Dorsoduro 3484/D 30123 Venezia 
  • Presentation
  • Scientific board

The series Culture del lavoro intends to bring out researches on work and its representations that take place at Ca’ Foscari and surroundings. The creation of at least one volume a year is ensured by the publication of the proceedings of the seminar Ascoltare il Lavoro that takes place in May at Ca’ Foscari University.
