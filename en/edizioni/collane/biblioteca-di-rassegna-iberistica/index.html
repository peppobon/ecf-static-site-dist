Biblioteca di Rassegna iberistica

  • TopicLinguistics 
  • Head office Univeristà Ca’ Foscari Venezia Dipartimento di Studi Linguistici e Culturali Comparati Ca’ Bernardo, Dorsoduro 3199 30123 Venezia, Italia
  • Presentation
  • Scientific board

The series Biblioteca di Rassegna Iberistica publishes monographs and collections of high scientific rigor essays regarding linguistic and cultural areas of Spanish, Spanish-American, Luso-Brazilian and Catalan. It is bound to present publications issued  from research activities of Ca’ Foscari University and foreign and italian institutuions’ and researchers’ publications. It aims to be a privileged location to discuss about research instruments of our subjects according to innovative theorical and historical perspectives. A summary and interdisciplinary project, publishes works about the whole angles of Iberic and Ibero-American culture.
