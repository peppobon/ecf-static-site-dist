Scientific certification of the works published by Edizioni Ca’ Foscari - Digital Publishing: all essays published in this volume have received a favourable opinion by at least two subject-matter experts, through an anonymous peer review process under the responsibility of the Scientific Committee of the series. The evaluations were conducted in adherence to the scientific and editorial criteria established by Edizioni Ca’ Foscari.

The Publishing House has a Scientific Committee that consists of 6 members, each one of them representing every Department and, therefore, every area of study (liberal arts, linguistic, economic and scientific). The Committee evaluates and judges all the new proposals of journals and series according to the following basic criteria:

  1. the series must have an Editor and a Scientific Committee, consisting mostly of senior researchers from other universities (preferably international), of proved scientific value;
  2. the series must display originality in subject matter and research method. 
  3. the series must have an Editorial Committee that handles the peer-review work flow following the Publishing House criteria and verifies the texts’ conformity with the editorial rules.

The editorial group works in order to guarantee the observance of these general guidelines.

The administration and the management commitee have control- and veto power over the individual scientific committees, once consulted them, in case obvious negligences are detected.

The Publishing House makes available a double and anonymous peer-review platform and offers steady support and up-to-date information regarding its use. The scientific evaluation standard recommended provides for a double anonymous evaluation performed by independent researchers, not belonging to the scientific committees.

Each Scientific Committee identifies its own reviewers. They are required to be experts in the series’ field of research and must have a considerable number of previous publications, which in turn have been submitted to a double and anonymous peer-review process. It is recommended that they don’t belong to the university that the series is affiliated to.

Within the platform, every series has a dedicated profile, managed by the director and the Scientific Committee.

Each paper is anonymously uploaded to the platform by the author and then assigned to a couple of reviewers by the scientific committee. The reviewers must establish whether the paper is “accepted” or “refused”. In case it is “accepted”, it must be distinguished if it is a) “accepted without reserve”, b) “accepted with request of little interventions” He must then fill in a form, which requires a judgment on several levels (Poor, Need Improvement, Average, Good, Excellent) about relevance, methodology, completeness, coherence and formal quality, and a comment for each of the above-mentioned fields.

The paper is rejected even if only one reviewer rejects it. In this case, the scientific Editor can decide to submit it to a third judgment.

If the reviewers request any intervention, the author must upload a second draft of the paper, which will be then approved by the series’ Editor.If the reviewers request any intervention, the author must upload a second draft of the paper, which will be then approved by the series’ Editor.

At the end of this process, the Publishing House verifies the paper’s compliance to the editorial guidelines and, if necessary, reserves the right to ask the Editorial Board for interventions in order to adapt the paper to the standards.

The process is, thus, ended and the evaluated and approved documents can proceed towards publication
