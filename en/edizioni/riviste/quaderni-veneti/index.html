The journal, founded by Giorgio Padoan as ‘organ’ of the Academic Centre for Venetian Studies, after fifty numbers on paper has decided to open to the digital platform of the Edizioni Ca' Foscari. Quaderni Veneti. Nuova serie digitale provided with an international scientific committee and a reading committee; it will retain its traditional biannual periodicity and will collect researches (written in all of the Western European culture languages) with philological, literary, linguistic and cultural-history nature related to the textual and cultural tradition of the Triveneto, from the Middle Ages to contemporary times.
