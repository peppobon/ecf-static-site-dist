English Literature was founded by the National Association of Teachers of English studies (ANDA) in 2014 and is presently run at Ca' Foscari University, Venice. The journal provides room for a critical analysis of issues and problems concerning English literature, also in a comparative view. The journal wants to promote a multidisciplinary approach to literature, to its relations with the various fields of knowledge, with culture, society, science, and the arts. It is also proposed as a privileged place for the discussion of the tools and strategies used by literature, according to various theoretical perspectives. The journal wants to promotes interaction among scholars at both an international and national level, and among teachers recognised in the various areas of specialisation and young researchers.
