Scienza e società

  • Head office  Dipartimento di Scienze Ambientali, Informatica e Statistica Università Ca’ Foscari Venezia Dorsoduro 2137 30121 Venezia
  • Presentation
  • Scientific board

The series, directed by Agostino Cortesi, collects researches on issues related to the social impact of science (values and ethics of science, scientific research policies, science economy, evaluation of the scientific production, the social impact of scientific training, technology transfer policies) and social impact issues that need a scientific approach for the formalization of analysis models and solution strategies. The series wants to enhance in particular texts that see the cooperation of scholars from different disciplines, in areas whose complexity requires the comparison and integration of different skills and methodologies.
