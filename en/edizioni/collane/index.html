General catalog

Topics Series Journals

Antichistica

The series, directed by Lucio Milano, is devoted to the studies of ancient and late-ancient world. It is divided into four sections: History and Epigraphy; Archeology; Oriental Studies; Philology and Literature.

Biblioteca di Rassegna iberistica

The series Biblioteca di Rassegna Iberistica publishes monographs and collections of high scientific rigor essays regarding linguistic and cultural areas of Spanish, Spanish-American, Luso-Brazilian and Catalan. 

Ca Foscari Japanese Studies

The series collects publications of high scientific rigor aimed at documenting the most original and theoretically advanced developments of the global Nipponese research, ranging from classicism to modernity. 

Culture del lavoro

The series Culture del lavoro intends to bring out researches on work and its representations that take place at Ca’ Foscari and surroundings. 

Diaspore

The series Diaspore. Quaderni di ricerca originates from the desire to investigate the human being’s diasporic dimension, in its various forms. 

Eurasiatica

The series was born to deal specifically with a wide area, composite but interrelated that, in addition to the traditional historical and cultural significance, is taking on an increasing political and economic value.

Filologie medievali e moderne

The series is currently divided into Serie occidentale e Serie orientale, which will gather researches of philological nature; its primary goal is to be the 'place' of exchange and of intellectual collaboration among scholars of the Western and Eastern traditions. 

Hilâl

La collana raccoglie monografie, edizioni di fonti e atti di convegni relativi alla storia del mondo turco-ottomano e dei popoli turco-mongoli.

I libri di Ca’ Foscari

Innesti/Crossroads

The series Innesti aims to investigate the role of narrative, literary and cinematographic forms, as well as the several discursive practices related to audiovisual media, in relation to the issues that define the contemporary horizon.

Italianistica

The present series provides a collection of the best work written by both professors and researchers of Ca' Foscari University in the field of Italian Studies. In particular, it aims to become the preferred means of publishing the most original doctoral theses by PhD students in Italian Literature. The series also includes in its scope proceedings of conferences, which have been held at Ca' Foscari, or miscellaneous collections relating to any aspect of Italian Studies and pertaining to Venetian culture.    

La prospettiva rovesciata | Obratnaja perspektiva

The series aims to collect proceedings of conventions, conferences and other important events promoted, within Ca’ Foscari, by csar (Center for Studies on Russia of Arts), in the field of the extraordinary cultural heritage that Russia, with examples from its past art and culture to its present tradition in the field of music, theater and cinema.

Philosophica

The series focuses on those texts in which philosophical research know how to compete with other disciplines (from the so-called "hard" sciences to the sciences of man and society) and other manifestations of human culture (art, literature, poetry, religion). 

Politiche sociali

The series is intended as a reference point on the research about the transformation of public policy processes in general and of welfare systems in particular.

Quaderni di Venezia Arti

La collana Quaderni di Venezia Arti accoglie saggi e contributi scientifici di studiosi italiani e stranieri, con particolare attenzione all’esito delle indagini sviluppate nell’ambito dei dottorati e attraverso le tesi di laurea magistrali.

Quaderni Veneti. Studi e ricerche

La collana è la ‘costola’ monografica della rivista Quaderni Veneti. Nuova serie digitale (QV): accoglie gli atti dei convegni organizzati dal Centro Interuniversitario di Studi veneti (CISVe) e edizioni e monografie relative alla tradizione testuale e culturale del Triveneto.

Ricerche per l’innovazione nell’industria automotive

SAIL

In the series, the volumes of which are approved by three blind referees before publication, are three types of search space: a. studies on the epistemologic nature of the science that studies language education, in the wider meaning that includes Italian mother tongue, second and foreign, modern languages and classical ones; b. operational studies on methods, strategies, language teaching methodologies; c. quantitative and qualitative surveys on particular aspects of language teaching in the various training areas. The collection hosts studies of scholars working both at Ca’ Foscari University and in other institutions.

Sapere l’Europa, sapere d’Europa

Sapere l’Europa, sapere d'Europa originates from the need to savor the news - some of them irreversible - of which the integration process is bearer and from which it feeds.

Scienza e società

The series, directed by Agostino Cortesi, collects researches on issues related to the social impact of science and social impact issues that need a scientific approach for the formalization of analysis models and solution strategies. 

Sinica venetiana

The series Sinica venetiana is devoted to the studies on ancient, modern and contemporary China. It collects monographs and critical editions of texts related to culture, history, art, economics, politics, international relations, environment, using an interdisciplinary approach. 

Società e trasformazioni sociali

The peer-reviewed book series Società e trasformazioni sociali reflect Ca’ Foscari scientific high standards and publishe online accessible books on pivotal social, political and economic issues of our time in a sociological perspective. 

Studi di archivistica, biblioteconomia, paleografia

The series aims to disseminate the researches relating to the enhancement of the cultural deposits of the region through joint works between teachers and young researchers in the field of the Master related, and to the material used for teaching purposes or teaching support.

Studi di Storia

La collana, coordinata da Laura Cerasi, Mario Infelise e Anna Rapetti, si propone di ospitare studi di storia medievale, moderna e contemporanea, spaziando tra la dimensione locale della storia veneziana e veneta e quella ampia della storia europea ed extraeuropea.
