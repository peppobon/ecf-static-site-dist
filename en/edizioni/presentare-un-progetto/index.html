Submitting a proposal

This section is devoted to Prospective Authors (for both scientific journals and book series), wishing to submit a book proposal to Edizioni Ca’ Foscari.

The proposal is examined by the ECF Scientific Committee, which will directly contact the representative suggested within the proposal.

We kindly ask you to provide all the information requested and an exhaustive description of the project’s scientific profile.

The projects can refer to both unprecedented or in print journals/book series.

For journal projects, it is necessary to specify the designated Scientific Editor and to provide his personal contacts. Should the journal be already active, it would be necessary to specify the Editor and the data concerning the enrollment by the Court.

The submitting of a proposal assumes that the Publishing House’s guidelines have been examined and accepted.
