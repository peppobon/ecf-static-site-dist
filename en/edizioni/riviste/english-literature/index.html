English Literature, founded by the National Association of Teachers of English studies (ANDA), provides room for a critical analysis of issues and problems concerning English literature and English language, also in a comparative view. The journal wants to promote a plural and interdisciplinary approach to literature, to its relations with the various fields of knowledge, with society, culture and science. It is also proposed as a privileged place for the discussion of the tools used to investigate the peculiar aspects of literature, according to various theoretical and historical perspectives. It promotes interaction among Anglistics' scholars at both an international and national level, and among teachers recognised in the various areas of specialisation and young researchers.
