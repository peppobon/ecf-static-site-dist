Abstract

This chapter aims at enlacing the correlation between new forms of employment and new vulnerabilities in the six countries analysed. In particular, the analysis done has pointed out the effects of new forms of employment on different generations of workers, through analyzing issues such as: the new forms of employment and the policies implemented in order to reduce the risk of labour market exclusion and increasing vulnerability.
